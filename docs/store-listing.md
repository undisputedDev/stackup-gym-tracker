## Full description — EN

```
The gym tracker that answers the only question that matters: what do I lift today?

StackUp's arrow system turns your reps into a decision:
▲ above your target range → more weight next time
▬ inside the range → the weight stays
▼ below the range → less weight next time

HOW IT WORKS
• Log your reps per set — the arrow appears as you type and shows what it means: "16 reps → next time 62.5 kg ▲"
• Finish the workout — StackUp remembers every arrow
• Next session, the adjusted weight is already filled in. No spreadsheets, no math at the squat rack.

SMART, NOT BLOATED
• Deload detection: three ▼ sessions in a row and StackUp suggests a real reset instead of another small step down
• Personal records: beat your all-time best and the finish summary celebrates it
• "Last time" on every exercise: 15/12/9 @ 60 kg — right where you need it
• Tap the arrow to override any suggestion. You stay in charge.

EVERYTHING YOU NEED, NOTHING YOU DON'T
• Ready-made splits (Upper/Lower, Push/Pull/Legs, Full Body) — fully editable
• Per-exercise rep ranges and weight increments
• Progress charts for every exercise
• Backup & restore — your data, your file
• kg or lbs, dark mode, English & German

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Free. No ads. No account. No cloud. Works offline — your training data never leaves your device.

Simple. Mature. Built for people who lift.
```
