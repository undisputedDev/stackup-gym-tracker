# Privacy Policy — StackUp

_Last updated: 23 July 2026_

StackUp ("the app") is a workout tracker for Android. This policy explains what data the
app handles. In short: **StackUp does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data.**

## Data we collect

**None.** StackUp does not require an account, sign-in, or any personal information to use.

## Data stored on your device

All the data you enter — your exercises, splits, workout sessions, weights, reps, and
settings — is stored **locally on your device** in a private app database (SQLite). This
data never leaves your device. It is not uploaded to us or to any third party.

When you uninstall the app, this local data is removed by the operating system.

## Data we share

**None.** We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to anyone. StackUp contains no
advertising SDKs and no third-party analytics.

## Network access

StackUp does not require an internet connection to function, and it does not send your
workout data anywhere. The app requests standard Android network permissions
(`INTERNET`, `ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE`) only so that system-provided services can operate —
specifically Google's official in‑app review prompt and standard platform diagnostics.
No personal or workout data is transmitted through these.

## Children's privacy

StackUp is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone,
including children.
